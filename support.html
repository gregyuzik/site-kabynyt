Frequently Asked Questions

📸

How do I add my first collectible?

Tap the «+» button. The camera opens straight to capture so you can shoot the item. Smart Add runs Identify on the photo and pre-fills the form before you tap save. You can also paste a title, scan a barcode, or type the details by hand.

📥

How do I import a collection?

Settings → Import → pick Goodreads (books), Letterboxd (movies), or Discogs (vinyl), then pick the CSV file. Each format expects the exporter's stock columns — nothing to configure. Covers back-fill automatically.

📊

Does barcode scanning need internet?

Yes — barcode scans send the digits to Google's Gemini 3.5 Flash for a deep lookup that returns title, manufacturer, year, and price range. Scanning still works offline, but you'll get the barcode digits only, not the title or cover.

✨

Does AI photo identification need internet?

Yes — Kabynyt sends the photo to Google's Gemini 3.5 Flash for identification. You can supply your own Gemini API key in Settings, or use the bundled key (free for 5 identifications, then $0.99/month).

☁️

How do I back up my collection?

iCloud sync is automatic when iCloud is enabled — every iPhone and iPad signed into the same Apple ID stays in sync through your private CloudKit container. Settings → Backup & Restore also exports a JSON snapshot you can save anywhere.

💰

Is Kabynyt free?

The app is free to download. All 41 categories, unlimited manual entry, photos, notes, CSV import, and iCloud sync are free forever. Smart Add is free for 5 identifications, then $0.99 a month for unlimited use. Cancel anytime.

🔎

Can I search for digital media without a barcode?

Yes — tap Search by name in the + menu and type a title. Kabynyt returns up to fifteen matches from iTunes (movies, TV, music, podcasts). Pick one and the form pre-fills.

🎨

Where do covers and metadata come from?

iTunes for movies/TV/music/podcasts, Open Library for books, MusicBrainz and Cover Art Archive for music, Discogs for vinyl, and Wikipedia for general entries. Only the title, identifier, or barcode is sent — never your collection.

🔍

How do filters and sorting work?

Filter by status (owned, wishlist, loaned, retired), photo presence, rating, or price; sort by date added, title, value, or rating. Filters apply across every category at once, or pin to one.

🏷️

What's the difference between tags and subcategories?

A category is one of 41 types Kabynyt knows the schema for (Books, Wine, LEGO, …). A subcategory is a free-form sub-bucket inside a category (“Pokémon” inside Trading Cards). A tag is any label you want, free-form, used across categories.

❤️

How do wishlist and loans work?

Mark any item as wishlist instead of owned to track what you want. Lend something? Tap Loaned and log who has it — the home tab surfaces overdue loans so you remember to ask for them back.

🗑️

I deleted something by accident

Settings → Recently Deleted. Items wait 30 days before they're purged. Tap an item to restore it to your collection exactly as it was.

Settings → Smart Add → paste your key. Bypass the 5-identification limit and pay Google directly per request.

🔎

iTunes search beats typing

For movies, TV, music, and podcasts, search by title returns covers and metadata in one tap.

🎨

Turn on iCloud sync before you add hundreds of items, so the first sync is small and fast.

Use wishlist for hunts

Troubleshooting

iCloud sync isn't working
Make sure iCloud Drive is enabled for Kabynyt in Settings → Apple ID → iCloud. Ensure you're signed into the same Apple ID on every device. The home tab shows a live sync status; pull to refresh if it's stuck.
Smart Add says “limit reached”
You've used the 5 bundled identifications. Either subscribe for $0.99/month in Settings, or paste your own Gemini API key in Settings → Smart Add and pay Google directly per request.
Photo identification picked the wrong item
Re-shoot in better light against a contrasting background — AI vision works best on clean shots. You can also edit the pre-filled form before tapping save, or fall back to barcode scan or title search.
Barcode scan returned the wrong title
Barcode databases sometimes ship the same UPC across re-issues. Tap the title to edit it, or use Search by name to find the right edition.
CSV import failed
Use the unmodified export from Goodreads, Letterboxd, or Discogs — Kabynyt expects the stock columns. If you edited the file, undo the changes or re-export.
Cover art didn't load on import
The back-fill runs on a background pass after import — give it a few minutes on a good connection. Pull to refresh on the category to re-trigger.
Subscription not recognized
Settings → Smart Add → Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the Apple ID used to subscribe.
I deleted an item; how do I get it back?
Settings → Recently Deleted. Items wait 30 days before they're purged. Tap an item to restore it.
