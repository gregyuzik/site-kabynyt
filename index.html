What it does

Made for the way you actually collect

A home tab that knows the date, five ways to add an item, CloudKit sync across your devices — all native to iOS 26.

Today, at a glance

The home tab surfaces date-aware picks — Halloween in October, the holidays in December — alongside Recently Added, Pick of the Day, and the live sync status. The collection greets you instead of waiting.

Scan a barcode

ISBN, UPC, EAN. One Gemini 3.5 Flash deep-lookup returns title, manufacturer, year, market price range, and the right category — in a single round trip.

Identify a photo

Snap a photo of a collectible. Gemini 3.5 Flash reads packaging text, sets, edition, year — and fills the form for you.

Search by name

For digital media without a barcode. Type a title, pick from up to 15 matches.

Type it

A plain manual form when you'd rather just enter what you know.

Import a CSV

Goodreads for books, Letterboxd for movies, Discogs for vinyl. Pick the file in Settings — Kabynyt back-fills covers automatically after import.

iCloud sync

Built for iPhone and iPad. CloudKit keeps every device in sync without you thinking about it.
