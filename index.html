The shortcuts that matter

✨

AI photo identify

Snap a collectible and Gemini 3.5 Flash reads packaging, set, edition, and year — then fills the form.

📊

Barcode deep-lookup

ISBN, UPC, EAN. One round trip returns title, manufacturer, year, and market price range.

📦

42 categories

Each understands its own field set, so the data you capture matches the thing you collect.

☁️

iCloud sync

CloudKit keeps every iPhone and iPad in sync, automatically, in your private container.

Features

🏠

Today, at a glance

A date-aware home tab with Recently Added, Pick of the Day, holiday-tinted picks, and live iCloud sync status.

📸

Camera-first +

Tap the «+» and the camera opens straight to capture — Kabynyt+ runs Identify on the photo and pre-fills the form before you tap save.

🔎

Search by name

For digital media without a barcode — type a title and pick from up to fifteen iTunes matches.

✏️

Type it

A plain manual form when you'd rather just enter what you know — every field optional except the title.

📥

Import a CSV

Goodreads for books, Letterboxd for movies, Discogs for vinyl. Pick the file — covers back-fill automatically.

🎨

Cover-art fetch

iTunes, Open Library, MusicBrainz, Discogs, and Wikipedia fill in covers and metadata after import.

❤️

Wishlist & loans

Flag items as wanted, log who borrowed what, and mark anything opened, loaned, or retired.

⭐

Ratings & value

Five-star ratings, purchase price, MSRP, and estimated value — the numbers that matter to a collector.

🏷️

Tags & subcategories

Free-form tags and subcategories (“Pokémon” inside Trading Cards, “Hot Wheels” inside Toys) you extend as you go.

🔍

Sort & filter

Filter by status, photos, rating, or price; sort by date, title, value, or rating — across every category.

🗑️

30-day trash

Deleted items wait 30 days in Recently Deleted before they're purged, so an accidental swipe is recoverable.

🔐

Private by design

SwiftData on-device, your private CloudKit container, no analytics, no trackers. Your collection is yours.
