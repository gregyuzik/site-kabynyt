Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 27, 2026

Our Privacy Promise

Kabynyt is owned, operated, and maintained by Gregory Yuzik (“us”, or “we”).

Your collection isn't just a feature of Kabynyt — it's the foundation. Kabynyt does not collect, store, transmit, or have access to your collection.

Every item you add — photos, notes, prices, metadata — lives in SwiftData on your iPhone or iPad. If you have iCloud enabled, it syncs through your private CloudKit container to your other Apple devices. Apple cannot read this data; Kabynyt cannot read this data; nobody but you can read this data.

What Kabynyt Collects

We want to be completely transparent: the app itself collects nothing about you. A few features hand specific data to third-party services on your behalf — those are listed below the table.

Data TypeCollected by Kabynyt?Details
Your collection❌ NoStored only on your device and your personal iCloud account.
Photos❌ NoPhotos you attach to items stay in your private CloudKit container.
Names or emails❌ NoKabynyt does not collect any personally identifiable information.
Usage analytics❌ NoKabynyt does not track how you use the app.
Advertising data❌ NoThere are no ads in Kabynyt and no advertising SDKs.
Location❌ NoKabynyt does not request location.

Data Providers

Some Kabynyt features query external services to identify items, look up barcodes, or fill in covers and metadata. Each request carries only what's needed for that lookup — never your collection, your other items, your name, your email, or anything that identifies you or your device. The table below lists every provider Kabynyt may contact and what each one receives.

ProviderUsed forWhat's sent
Google GeminiPhoto identification, barcode deep-lookup, AI-assisted name searchThe photo, barcode digits, or text query you initiated
Apple iTunes SearchMovies, TV, music, podcastsYour title query or an Apple catalog ID
Open LibraryBooksYour title query, ISBN, or work identifier
MusicBrainzMusic releases and metadataYour artist/title query or a MusicBrainz release ID
Cover Art ArchiveMusic cover imagesA MusicBrainz release ID
DiscogsVinyl recordsYour title query or a Discogs release ID
WikipediaGeneral catalog entriesYour title query or a Wikipedia page slug

Each provider's privacy policy governs how it handles the request after it leaves your device. Kabynyt does not retain photos, queries, or any other lookup data, and we do not see this traffic — requests go from your device directly to the provider.

Your Data Stays Yours

All of your content is stored exclusively on your device and in your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit. Your iCloud data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy and protected by Apple's encryption.

Device Permissions

Kabynyt may request the following device permissions. No data from these permissions is ever sent to Kabynyt:

  • Camera — Used to photograph collectibles and scan barcodes.
  • Photo Library — Used to pick existing photos for items.
  • Files — Used to pick CSV files for import and to write JSON snapshots for backup.

Apple Platform Services

  • CloudKit — Syncs your collection through your personal iCloud account.
  • StoreKit — Handles the Kabynyt+ subscription. Kabynyt never receives your payment details.

For more information, see Apple's Privacy Policy.

Subscriptions

Kabynyt+ is an optional auto-renewable subscription processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. Kabynyt does not have access to your payment information.

Children's Privacy

Kabynyt does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Your Rights (International)

Regardless of where you live, Kabynyt's privacy-first architecture means your rights are inherently protected:

  • Right of Access — All your data is on your device and in your personal iCloud account.
  • Right to Rectification — You can edit or update any item directly within the app.
  • Right to Erasure — Delete items within the app or delete the app itself.
  • Right to Data Portability — Settings → Backup & Restore exports a JSON snapshot of your entire collection.
  • Right to Object — Since Kabynyt performs no processing of your data, there is nothing to object to.

For EEA users, these rights are guaranteed under the GDPR. For Brazilian users, under the LGPD.

For any privacy-related inquiries, contact us at support@kabynyt.com.

Website Analytics

This website (kabynyt.com) is a static site hosted on GitHub Pages and served through Cloudflare. It does not use cookies and does not run any analytics. Standard server logs (IP, user agent, page requested) are retained by GitHub and Cloudflare on their standard retention schedules. The Kabynyt app itself does not include analytics either.
