Common questions

How do I import an existing collection?

Settings → Import → pick a CSV. Kabynyt asks you to map your columns to its fields the first time; it remembers the mapping for subsequent imports.

Does barcode scanning need internet?

Yes — barcode scans send the barcode digits to Google's Gemini 3.5 Flash for a deep lookup that returns title, manufacturer, year, and price range. Scanning still works offline, but you'll get the barcode only, not the title or cover.

Does AI photo identification need internet?

Yes — Kabynyt sends the photo to Google's Gemini 3.5 Flash for identification. You can supply your own Gemini API key in Settings, or use the bundled key (limited to 5 free identifications, then $0.99/month).

How do I back up my collection?

iCloud sync is automatic if enabled. Settings → Backup also exports a JSON snapshot you can save anywhere.

I deleted something by accident

Settings → Trash. Deleted items live for 30 days before they're purged.
