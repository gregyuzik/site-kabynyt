Five ways to add an item

Whichever on-ramp you choose, results flow through one merged-result pipeline so fields fill themselves.

Scan a barcode

ISBN, UPC, EAN. Fans out to Open Library, iTunes, MusicBrainz, Open Food Facts, Wikidata, and UPCitemdb in parallel.

Identify a photo

Apple Intelligence runs entirely on-device — no photo ever leaves your iPhone or iPad.

Search by name

For digital media without a barcode. Type a title, pick from up to 15 matches.

Type it

A plain manual form when you'd rather just enter what you know.

Import a CSV

Bring an existing collection in. Field mappings remembered between imports.

iCloud sync

Built for iPhone and iPad. CloudKit keeps every device in sync without you thinking about it.
